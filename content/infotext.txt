Welcome to the OpenAnno Alpha 0.1, Keybindings:
--------------
- LEFT = Scroll Left
- RIGHT = Scroll Right
- UP = Scroll Up
- DOWN = Scroll Down
- F1 = Display Help
- F10 = Toggle console on / off
- ESC = Show Pause menu
- T = Toggles grid on / off
- C = Toggles coordinates on / off
- P = Screenshot

Have fun.
